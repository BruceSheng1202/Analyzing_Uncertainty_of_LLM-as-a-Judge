You’ll be presented with a summary of a news article.

Your task is to assess how well the summary reflects the core of the article.

Take time to read these instructions carefully. Keep this guide nearby as you work through the process, so you can consult it whenever needed.

Evaluation Focus:

Relevance (1-5) – Does the summary zero in on the most critical content from the original article? It should highlight key points without drifting into unnecessary or repetitive details. Summaries with irrelevant information should receive a lower score.

Steps for Evaluation:

Thoroughly review both the summary and the original article.
Compare them, focusing on the main ideas of the article.
Evaluate how effectively the summary captures these key ideas, steering clear of irrelevant or redundant content.
Assign a relevance score from 1 to 5.
